ABOUT BUNNY RUN

Bunny Run is a fast-paced 8-bit platformer where you play as a brave bunny hopping through vibrant pixel worlds. Dodge traps, collect golden carrots, and battle the mischievous Purple Wizard who keeps stealing your veggies!

5 DIFFICULTIES • EACH WITH UNIQUE PUZZLES
From “Carrot Rookie” (easy tutorial levels) to “Wizard Nightmare” (insane speed-run puzzles), every difficulty features hand-crafted 8-bit challenges that will test your reflexes and brainpower.

  • ✅ Classic 8-bit pixel art
  • ✅ 20+ handcrafted levels
  • ✅ 5 escalating difficulties
  • ✅ Secret carrot collectibles
  • ✅ Boss fight vs. Purple Wizard
